## 📰 媒体覆盖：Science Advances 研究引发关注

**EurekAlert 新闻发布 (2025-05-14)**  
来源：City St George's, University of London

### 研究亮点引用

> "Most research so far has treated LLMs in isolation... but real-world AI systems will increasingly involve many interacting agents. We wanted to know: can these models coordinate their behaviour by forming conventions, the building blocks of a society? The answer is yes, and what they do together can't be reduced to what they do alone."
> — **Ariel Flint Ashery**, 首席研究员，City St George's

### 关于集体偏见的洞见

> "Bias doesn't always come from within... we were surprised to see that it can emerge between agents—just from their interactions. This is a blind spot in most current AI safety work, which focuses on single models."
> — **Andrea Baronchelli**, 复杂性科学教授，City St George's，资深作者

### 研究意义总结

> "This study opens a new horizon for AI safety research. It shows the depth of the implications of this new species of agents that have begun to interact with us—and will co-shape our future. Understanding how they operate is key to leading our coexistence with AI, rather than being subject to it. We are entering a world where AI does not just talk—it negotiates, aligns, and sometimes disagrees over shared behaviours, just like us."
> — **Andrea Baronchelli**

### 脆弱性发现

研究还揭示了这些涌现规范的**脆弱性**：
- 小型、坚定的 AI 代理群体可以推动整个群体转向新的命名规范
- 呼应了人类社会中著名的"临界点"效应或"临界质量"动态
- 结果在四种不同类型的 LLM 中都具有稳健性

## 📊 CSCW 2025：多智能体系统塑造亲社会规范

**会议：** ACM CSCW 2025 Companion  
**论文：** Multi-Agent Systems Shape Social Norms for Prosocial Behavior Change

### 研究问题
探索多智能体系统是否可以建立"虚拟社会规范"来鼓励捐赠行为。

### 实验设计
- 在线实验，参与者与一组代理讨论捐赠行为
- 测量指标：感知社会规范、从众性、捐赠行为、用户体验
- 对比：内群体代理 vs 外群体代理

### 核心发现
1. **多智能体交互有效增加**感知社会规范和捐赠意愿
2. **内群体代理**产生更强的感知社会规范、更高的从众性和更大的捐赠增长
3. **社会认同动态**可用于促进虚拟环境中的亲社会行为

### 对 OMP 的启示
- 代理的"群体身份"设计影响用户的行为改变效果
- 内群体/外群体动态在 AI 代理-人类交互中同样适用
- 多代理讨论可作为有效的社会规范干预手段
